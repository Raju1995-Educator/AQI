This device monitors the air quality at PVM School to ensure a healthy learning environment for students. Below is what each value means:

  • Temperature (¬∞C): Measures the ambient heat level in the classroom.
  • Humidity (%): The amount of water vapor in the air. High humidity can feel "sticky," while low humidity can cause dry skin.
  • CO2 Concentration (PPM): Carbon Dioxide measured in Parts Per Million. High levels indicate poor ventilation.
  • Vo (Voltage): The raw electrical output from the MQ135 sensor. Higher voltage indicates higher detectable pollutants.
  • AQI Score: A calculated index from 0 to 500 summarizing overall air safety.
